MvcSiteMapProvider information

Congratulations, you have just installed the enhanced MvcSiteMapProvider!

The enhanced MvcSiteMapProvider now includes the ability to produce a /video-sitemap.xml (tailored video sitemap) to submit to Google. And, it includes the ability to leave the canonical URL on for the master page.
